6. COOKIE POLICY


A cookie is a text file that your web browser receives from a web server and stores on your computer. Session and permanent cookies are the two kinds of cookies that are helpful to distinguish. Session cookies are distinct from permanent cookies in that they are temporary, expire, and are usually erased when the browser is closed. On the other hand, persistent cookies are kept on your computer after you close your tab and stay there until they expire or you erase them. Except for Internet Services that need you to log in, such as our client extranets and a Region Code cookie that exists over time and guarantees that you are led to the right version of our website, the firm uses session cookies in conjunction with its Internet Services. Cookies are used for the firm’s Internet Services. Session cookies are used by the company in conjunction with its Internet Services, primarily for cybersecurity purposes, to personalize your experience, such as highlighting recently viewed items, detecting and remembering your region and language preferences, and tracking your activities on the Internet Services.
